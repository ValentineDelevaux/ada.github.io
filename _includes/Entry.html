Welcome dear visitor

Welcome, visitor. My name is Viktor, and I am the butler of this grand castle. You’ve come at an interesting time, as my master, Count Dracula, has turned part of his castle into a museum dedicated to horror movies.

Dracula, as you may know, is not just a legendary figure, but also an actor in the world of horror films. He has lived for centuries and has seen how both society and horror films have changed over time. He’s always been fascinated by how movies show our deepest fears and how those fears evolve with the world around us.

At the moment, Dracula is resting. He’s working hard on his next movie, *Dracula and the Eight-Headed Shark*. It’s keeping him very busy, as he prepares for his new role.

But enough about that. Please, follow me, and I will show you around the museum. It’s a place where you can learn about the history of horror films through Dracula’s eyes. It’s a strange and exciting world where the past and future of fear come together.

Come with me, and let me show you this fascinating collection.
